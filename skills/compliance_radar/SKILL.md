# Compliance Radar: UAE Regulatory Intelligence

## Skill Description
Uses Gemini 3 Pro with Grounding to monitor real-time regulatory shifts, ensuring Deriv is never surprised by AML law changes.

## Real-World Implementation
- **Grounding Tool:** Searches UAE Executive Office for Control and Non-Proliferation (EOCN) updates.
- **Verification:** Flags transactions involving newly sanctioned entities or high-risk regions in real-time.
- **Audit Trail:** Provides citations for every regulatory check to aid human investigation.
